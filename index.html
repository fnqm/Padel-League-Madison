League Guidelines Winter 2026

1 League Structure

  • Players: 8 core players + 2 official substitutes
  • Match Day: Tuesdays
  • Time: 5:308:30 PM
    • Match 1: 5:307:00 (4 players)
    • Match 2: 7:008:30 (4 players)
  • Season Length:
    • 4 Tuesdays (Feb 10, 17, 24 & Mar 3) Regular Season
    • 2 Tuesdays (Mar 10 & 17) Playoffs

2 Regular Season Format

  • 3 sets per match
  • Partners alternate every set
  • Players for each match and set are selected randomly, with the goal of ensuring players compete with different partners and opponents as much as possible throughout the season; the organizer may make minor adjustments to minimize repeated combinations
  • Players must track games won and lost
Standings & Ranking: Rankings are based on total games won (not sets won). Tiebreaker: number of matches won.

3 Scoring Rules

  • Deuce: one deuce only, followed by a Golden Point
  • 66 in a set: standard tiebreak to 7, win by 2

4 Playoffs

  • Playoffs are seeded based on regular-season rankings

  • Winners advance to the Final
  • Losers play a 3rd4th place match
  • Playoff matches: best of 3 sets

5 Substitution Policy

  • Players must notify the group chat at least 3 days in advance if they cannot attend
  • The two official substitutes have first priority to fill the open spot
  • If neither official substitute is available, external substitutes may be contacted
  • If no substitute can be found, the match will be rescheduled as a last resort

6 General Conduct

  • Players are responsible for accurate score reporting (Google Sheet will be shared with all players)
  • Sportsmanship and fair play are expected at all times
